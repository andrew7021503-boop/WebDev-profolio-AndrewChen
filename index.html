Ingredients:

  • Ground pork
  • Napa cabbage
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Soy sauce and sesame oil
  • Dumpling wrappers

How to make dumplings:

  1. Mix the pork, cabbage, and seasonings to make the filling.
  2. Place a small amount of filling in the center of each wrapper.
  3. Fold and seal the dumplings to prevent leaks.
  4. Boil or steam the dumplings until fully cooked.
