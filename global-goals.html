UN Sustainable Development Goals

Goal 2: Zero Hunger

End hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture.

The UN Global Goal I am most interested in is Zero Hunger. I believe this is the simplest goal compare to other goals, and no one should go to bed hungry, and everyone deserves access to safe, nutritious and sufficient food.

As a student(a computer science student), I know that we can use technology to support this goal, For example building websites that raise awareness, share knowledge about sustainable farming, and even connect communities with food resources.

Achieving Zero Hunger requires global cooperation, innovation and long-term commitment. I will continue developing my skills (including everything I learned)so that I can maybe contribute to projects that make a real difference in people’s lives.
